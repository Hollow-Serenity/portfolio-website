ZeroXess - The Project from Kumbaya

As a future software engineer you acquire knowledge and skills that others do not have. It gives you the opportunity, but also the responsibility to use it in such a way that you make the world a little more beautiful. During semester 1 you were allowed to come up with a project yourself that has to do with the UN sustainable development goals. During this project you will work for Kumbaya, an organization whose mission is to give marginalized populations access to the digital world.
In this rapidly globalized and technologically advanced era, information and knowledge are the main drivers of economic and sustainable development. Nobody should be left behind. Yet there are still 3.6 billion people who do not have access to information. Right now, in the midst of the Corona crisis, information is of vital importance. Kumbaya has developed the Kumba zeroXess to give marginalized population groups access to information.
The zeroXess consists of leading technology, affordable and compact. With a little bit of sun, the zeroXess brings power, TV, radio, broadband internet, even CO2 and weather sensors and a lot more to off-grid populations, desperate refugee camps and areas hit by natural disasters.
